//ABOUT


My name is Jim. I'm a Creative Director, teacher and writer of various things.


I've had the good fortune of working on famous brands with smart, kind, talented people. My work has been recognized at award shows, but I take the most satisfaction in making content that delights people in the real world.


I've also taught writing, concepting and storytelling classes and co-author a blog for students and juniors. I love to see young creatives realize their dreams. I can lead a department, lead a project, lead a new business pitch or work as a solo copywriter. I can work with an agency team, imbed at a client, lead multi-agency collaborations or any mix of the those. Short term, long term, strategy through execution, I'm up for anything. My goal is simple: to make great work with great people.


Thanks for taking a look at my work. Please reach out if I can help you in any way.
