//ABOUT

My name is Jim. I'm a freelance writer, creative director and teacher.

I've had the good fortune of working with smart, kind, talented people on a wide range of famous brands. Some of my work has been recognized by award shows. Personally, I take the most satisfaction in making content that delights people in the real world.

I also teach writing, concepting and storytelling classes and co-author a blog for students and juniors. Unfortunately, true mentorship and investment in building young talent is increasingly rare in the agency world. I love to see young creatives break through and realize their dreams, big and small. I try to help where I can.

I can lead a department, lead a project, lead a new business pitch or work as a solo copywriter. I can work with an agency team, work from afar, imbed at a client, lead multi-agency collaborations or any combination. Short term, long term, strategy through execution. My goal is simple: to make great work with great people.

Thanks for stopping by. Please reach out if I can help in any way.
